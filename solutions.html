Tiered Delivery Models

1) SaaS / Portal (Cloud-Hosted by Virelia)

Revenue Model: subscriptions + consulting hours

  • Best for: startups, SMEs, NGOs, universities, nonprofits, enterprises wanting to test cheaply.
  • Value: lowest entry cost, fastest to start, no infra required.
  • Risk: some corporations (esp. healthcare, defense) won’t push sensitive data through your servers.

2) Hybrid / Managed Deployment (Client Cloud or Private Tenant): NOT YET AVAILABLE

Revenue Model: setup fee + managed service contract.

  • Best for: mid/large enterprises with compliance needs (HIPAA, GDPR, banking, defense contractors).
  • Value: you spin up a private tenant instance on their AWS/Azure/GCP account.
  • Risk: more setup/maintenance work, but gives them security control.

3) On-Premise / Self-Hosted (Air-Gapped Option): NOT YET AVAILABLE

Revenue Model: one-time licensing/support contract + optional long-term maintenance agreements.

  • Best for: defense, government, highly regulated industries (finance, critical infrastructure).
  • Value: client runs Virelia backend on their own infrastructure (even mainframes or air-gapped networks).
  • Risk: ou lose recurring SaaS revenue; harder to support updates.
