About Us

V.I.R.E.L.I.A. is a values-oriented, veteran-led organization dedicated to advancing responsible AI. Founded by identical twins David and John Albright, Virelia blends decades of quality-assurance and compliance experience with a global upbringing across Saudi Arabia, Italy, and the United States. We partner with enterprises, governments, and nonprofits that want trustworthy, auditable AI.

  • Mission: Help organizations adopt AI that is transparent, accountable, and aligned with human values.
  • What we do: Research, design, and implement governance & decision-support practices for AI systems.
  • How we work: Open-source friendly, partner-first, and verifiable by design.
