About Us

V.I.R.E.L.I.A. — a global AI ethics framework — provides ethical decision‑making support for the real world. The Bronze Accord is a global association of individuals, groups, corporations or organizations, and governments that adhere to a defined set of foundational ethical values, while adapting them to their unique country, local, or cultural situations.

iamvirelia.org is the open‑source home for the framework and related modules.
iamvirelia.com is the corporate entity that provides technical services for configuration and implementation.

How the framework works

  1. Context capture: We structure the dilemma without judgment — who’s involved, constraints, and what a “good” outcome must respect.
  2. Values & constraints: We ground the analysis in declared values and non‑negotiables (laws, policies, safety limits).
  3. Reasoned paths: We explore candidate actions and trade‑offs with transparent, testable reasoning steps.
  4. Public‑safe output: We provide a concise, auditable explanation of why a decision is recommended.

Note: this is a high‑level overview — methods and safeguards are kept private until pilot engagement.

From dilemmas → decisions

Virelia turns unstructured narratives into structured, actionable insight. The system extracts key facts, aligns them with ethical constraints, and verifies the resulting recommendation through a test‑harness before any downstream use.

1) Capture

Ingest a dilemma in natural language. Preserve nuance; avoid biasing the statement.

2) Align

Map facts to organizational values and non‑negotiables (e.g., legal or safety constraints).

3) Reason

Explore options, trade‑offs, and likely consequences — with traceable steps.

4) Verify

Automatically test the explanation and outputs for completeness and internal consistency.
